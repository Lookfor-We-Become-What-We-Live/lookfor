# Welcome to Lookfor!

Lookfor is a mobile application designed to facilitate friendships by connecting loosely associated groups of people through curated real-world experiences. By leveraging a network of trusted business partners as venues for these interactions, Lookfor enables a seamless transition from digital discovery to genuine human connection.

Our mission is to simplify modern relationships by focusing on shared moments rather than digital fatigue.

**Stop texting. Just experience.**
