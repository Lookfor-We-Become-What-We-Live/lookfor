# Welcome to Lookfor!
Lookfor is a mobile application designed to facilitate friendships by connecting loosely associated groups of people with curated real-world experiences. By leveraging a network of business partners as the venues for these interactions, Lookfor provides the users with a seamless transition from digital discovery to genuine human connection. Our mission is to simplify modern relationships by focusing on shared moments rather than digital fatigue. Stop texting. Just experience.
